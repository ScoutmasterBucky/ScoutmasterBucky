List of Merit Badges

Find information and resources on each of the 141 Scouts BSA merit badges offered by Scouting America. Biannual updates are released during the first quarter and third quarter of the year.

The links below direct to the specific merit badge and its requirements as well as links to resources like the Scoutmaster Bucky Merit Badge Workbook and Class Preparation Pages.

All requirements are current as listed on Scouting America's List unless otherwise noted.
