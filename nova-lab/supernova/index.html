Supernova Awards

These awards are ways to reward Scouts for learning more about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) subjects. The Supernova awards are earned as the next step after achieving Nova awards. There are different Nova awards for each program: Cub Scouts, Scouts BSA, and Venturing and Sea Scouts.

Supernova Award Progression

  • Cub Scouts
    • (Bronze) Bear and Wolf Cub Scouts can earn the Dr. Luis Walter Alvarez Supernova Award.
    • (Bronze) Webelos and Arrow of Light Scouts have the Dr. Charles H. Townes Supernova Award.
  • Scouts BSA
    • (Bronze) Earn the Dr. Bernard Harris Supernova Award first.
    • (Silver) The Thomas Alva Edison Supernova Award is able to be earned after the bronze.
  • Venturers and Sea Scouts
    • (Bronze) Start with the bronze Dr. Sally Ride Supernova Award.
    • (Silver) You may work on the Wright Brothers Supernova Award after earning the Scouts BSA or Venturing / Sea Scouts bronze.
    • (Gold) The Dr. Albert Einstein Supernova Award can be worked on after the Scouts BSA or Venturing / Sea Scouts silver.
