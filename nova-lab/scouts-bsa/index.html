Scouts BSA Nova Awards

There are numerous adventures one can experience while earning the medals that are awarded in the Scouts BSA program.

Science

  • Let It Grow! - Explore where your food comes from and how agriculture affects your life each day.
  • Mendel's Minions - Explore how genetic information affects your life every day.
  • Shoot! - Explore how science affects your life each day.
  • Splash! - Explore how water affects your life every day.

Technology

  • Hello, World - Explore the world of coding or computer programming.
  • Start Your Engines! - Explore how technology affects your life each day.

Engineering

  • Next Big Thing - Explores the world of product design, which involves balancing time, cost, and quality to create something new
  • Up and Away - Explores the world of fluid dynamics, and how it affects your everyday life.
  • Whoosh! - Explore how engineering affects your life each day.

Math

  • Designed to Crunch - Explore how math affects your life each day.
