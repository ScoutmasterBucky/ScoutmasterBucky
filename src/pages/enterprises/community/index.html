Community

Outreach, summer camps, and youth development — powered by our stores and our members.

Community Outreach Programs

Our outreach programs are supervised day programs designed to develop teamwork, confidence, and environmental awareness in young people. Modeled after many Scouting America merit badge areas, these programs introduce kids to the outdoors in structured, supportive settings.

Participants explore activities including:

  • Nature Study
  • Insect Study
  • Hiking
  • Geocaching
  • Biking
  • Running
  • Swimming

Each year, our stores collectively contribute thousands of volunteer hours to local communities — staff and customers working together to make outdoor experiences accessible to everyone.

Summer Camp

Our summer camp is a full-week immersive experience for youth ages 11 to 16. Campers participate in a structured rotation of outdoor activities — from rock climbing and canoeing to orienteering and wilderness skills — all guided by trained staff.

Camp isn't just for participants. We also bring on youth counselors ages 15 to 18 who help run daily activities, mentor younger campers, and gain hands-on experience in a professional setting. Many of our counselors go on to join the Scoutmaster Bucky team in retail or program roles.

Every camper leaves with new skills, stronger confidence, and friendships built around shared challenge. Every counselor leaves with leadership experience that shows up on college applications and first résumés.

Membership That Makes a Difference

Our membership program is different. It doesn't come with discounts — it comes with impact.

What Members Receive

  • —Videos, photos, and write-ups showing how membership fees are helping kids across the nation
  • —Newsletters written by the youth who participate in our programs
  • —Quarterly impact reports with stories from camp, outreach events, and leadership pipeline milestones

Where the Money Goes

  • —Funding outreach programs that introduce kids to nature, teamwork, and environmental responsibility
  • —Supporting summer camp scholarships for families who couldn't otherwise afford the experience
  • —Equipping youth counselors and pipeline participants with training, mentorship, and career development

When you become a member, you're not buying a discount card. You're investing in a young person's future.
