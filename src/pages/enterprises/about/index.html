About Us

Equip people for the outdoors while investing in the leaders of tomorrow.

What We're About

Bucky Enterprises is a national outdoor retailer and excursion outfitter headquartered in Richfield, Minnesota. We sell camping gear, mountain bikes, climbing equipment, canoes, and everything else you need to get outside. But gear is only part of the story.

We also run climbing classes with indoor and outdoor walls, rent equipment for people who want to try before they buy, and help plan high-adventure excursions with maps, national park permits, and online scheduling.

What sets us apart is our commitment to youth development and community investment. The profits from our retail operations — combined with generous donations from our members — fund community outreach programs, summer camps, and career pathways for young people across the country.

Our Core Values

Integrity First
We do the right thing — even when nobody's watching. Honest pricing, transparent practices, and accountability at every level.
Service Over Sales
We'd rather help a customer find exactly what they need than push a more expensive option. Trust earns loyalty.
Preparedness & Responsibility
The outdoors demands respect. We equip customers with the right knowledge and gear so they're ready for whatever comes next.
Community Investment
Our profits fund outreach, camps, and youth programs. We believe businesses should give back to the communities they serve.
Growth Through Leadership
We develop leaders — starting with the young people who work in our stores and participate in our programs.

Youth Leadership Pipeline

Our Youth Leadership Pipeline is a structured program that gives young people hands-on professional experience while they're still in school. Many of our store managers and regional coordinators started as summer hires.

Pipeline participants gain real-world skills in:

  • —Retail Operations: customer engagement, merchandising, point-of-sale systems
  • —Community Engagement: event planning, volunteer coordination, outreach facilitation
  • —Logistics & Inventory: supply chain fundamentals, stock management, data tracking
  • —Customer Experience Analysis: feedback collection, service improvement, satisfaction metrics
  • —Program Leadership: camp counseling, mentorship, program design and delivery

Our People Are Our Strength

Much of our staff started as the very youth we serve. We hire interns, part-time students, and summer workers — then keep them as part of the Bucky Family with competitive pay, strong company values, and a genuine commitment to employee well-being.

We don't just talk about developing leaders. We build careers, from first jobs to full-time management, right here at Bucky Enterprises.
