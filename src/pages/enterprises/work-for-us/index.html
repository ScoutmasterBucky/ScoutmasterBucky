Work For Us

Start your career with purpose. We're hiring youth for summer, part-time, and flexible positions across the country.

Join the Bucky Family

At Bucky Enterprises, many of our team members started as the youth we serve. We hire students and young adults, give them real responsibilities, and invest in their growth with competitive pay, strong values, and genuine care.

No special skills or prior experience required for any of the positions below. We'll train you, mentor you, and — if you're a good fit — keep you as part of the team for as long as you want to stay.

Open Positions

All current positions are designed for students and young adults. No experience necessary.

Retail Adventure Specialist

Summer Seasonal
>💰 $15.50 – $17.00/hr🕐 30–40 hrs/week, June through August

Bucky Enterprises is seeking high-energy, customer-focused students to join our retail team during our busiest season. As a Retail Adventure Specialist, you will work directly with customers to outfit them for camping, hiking, water sports, and backyard adventures. You'll learn product lines, help customers choose the right gear for their skill level and trip plans, and keep the sales floor organized and inviting.

Responsibilities & Duties

  • Greet and assist customers on the sales floor, helping them select camping, hiking, and water sport equipment
  • Maintain product displays, restock shelves, and ensure accurate signage and pricing
  • Operate point-of-sale systems and process transactions accurately
  • Participate in weekly product training sessions to stay current on gear features and recommendations
  • Support inventory counts and help receive shipments during peak delivery periods
  • Assist with in-store events, gear demonstrations, and seasonal promotions

Who Fits This Role Best

  • Genuine enthusiasm for outdoor activities and helping people try new things
  • Strong verbal communication skills and comfort approaching customers
  • Reliability — consistent attendance and punctuality matter in a seasonal role
  • Willingness to learn product details and share that knowledge confidently
  • Ability to stand for extended periods and lift up to 40 lbs
  • Team-oriented attitude with a positive, solutions-first mindset

Why You'd Want This Job

This is a fast-paced, team-oriented environment that builds real customer service and business skills. High-performing seasonal employees are often invited to continue part-time during the school year or considered for team lead opportunities the following summer. Employee discount included.

Community Outreach Event Crew Member

Summer + Select Weekends
>💰 $14.75 – $16.25/hr🕐 15–25 hrs/week, flexible around school schedule

Bucky Enterprises hosts service projects, gear demonstrations, and youth engagement events throughout the region. Outreach Crew Members help represent the company at these programs. You'll assist with event setup, interact with families and community groups, distribute information about our programs, and help facilitate hands-on gear demos and outdoor skill stations.

Responsibilities & Duties

  • Set up and break down event equipment including tents, tables, signage, and demo gear
  • Engage with event attendees, answer questions about Bucky programs, and distribute materials
  • Assist with interactive demonstrations such as tent setup races, compass navigation, and knot-tying stations
  • Coordinate with community partners and venue contacts before and during events
  • Track attendance and collect participant feedback using provided forms
  • Represent the company professionally and enthusiastically at all public-facing events

Who Fits This Role Best

  • Comfortable speaking to groups and engaging with people of all ages
  • Interest in community service, public outreach, or event planning
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently when needed
  • Organized and able to manage multiple tasks during fast-paced events
  • Physically able to lift and carry event supplies (up to 35 lbs)
  • Reliable transportation to event locations within the region

Why You'd Want This Job

This role is ideal for students interested in communications, public service, or marketing. You'll build real-world event coordination and public speaking skills while representing a company that genuinely gives back. Top performers may be invited to join our Youth Leadership Advisory Panel.

Junior Inventory & Logistics Coordinator

Part-Time Year-Round
>💰 $15.00 – $17.50/hr🕐 12–20 hrs/week, evenings and weekends available

The Logistics Team at Bucky Enterprises manages high-value inventory across retail locations. We are hiring detail-oriented students to support receiving, tracking, and organizing merchandise. You'll work behind the scenes to make sure product moves efficiently from delivery truck to sales floor, using barcode scanning systems, inventory software, and physical organization techniques.

Responsibilities & Duties

  • Receive incoming shipments, verify contents against purchase orders, and log discrepancies
  • Scan and catalog merchandise into the inventory management system
  • Organize backstock areas and maintain clean, labeled storage sections
  • Assist with weekly cycle counts and quarterly full-store inventory audits
  • Coordinate with sales floor staff to prioritize restocking based on demand
  • Flag damaged or defective items and process them according to company procedure

Who Fits This Role Best

  • Strong attention to detail and comfort working with data and numbers
  • Ability to work independently in a stockroom environment with minimal supervision
  • Familiarity with basic computer software (spreadsheets, email, web-based tools)
  • Physical ability to lift boxes up to 50 lbs and stand for extended periods
  • Organized, methodical approach to tasks — accuracy matters more than speed
  • Interest in business operations, supply chain, or analytical work

Why You'd Want This Job

Accurate inventory ensures customers get what they need when they need it. This position builds operational and analytical skills valuable in business, supply chain, or engineering careers. Advancement to Inventory Lead is possible within 12–18 months, and year-round availability means you'll develop deeper expertise than a seasonal role allows.

Summer Expedition Program Assistant

Full-Time Summer
>💰 $16.00 – $18.00/hr🕐 40 hrs/week, Monday–Friday, mid-June through mid-August

Bucky Enterprises operates skill-building day camps focused on outdoor readiness and teamwork. The Summer Expedition Program Assistant supports lead instructors in supervising groups of 8–12 middle school participants. You'll help facilitate daily activities — from orienteering and fire safety to climbing and water skills — while keeping campers safe, engaged, and having fun.

Responsibilities & Duties

  • Assist lead instructors with daily activity preparation, delivery, and wrap-up
  • Supervise assigned camper groups during transitions, meals, and free time
  • Help teach and demonstrate outdoor skills including knot-tying, compass navigation, and basic first aid
  • Monitor participant safety and enforce program rules consistently and fairly
  • Maintain and organize program equipment and report any damage or shortages
  • Communicate daily with parents during drop-off and pick-up, providing updates on their camper's experience

Who Fits This Role Best

  • Previous experience working with children or youth in any setting (school, sports, tutoring, church, etc.)
  • Patience, enthusiasm, and a calm demeanor under pressure
  • Comfort with outdoor environments, weather variability, and physical activity
  • Ability to follow structured lesson plans while adapting to group needs
  • Current CPR/First Aid certification (or willingness to obtain before start date — we'll cover the cost)
  • Strong sense of responsibility and awareness of youth safety protocols

Why You'd Want This Job

This is a competitive entry point into our Youth Leadership Pipeline. Successful candidates may be considered for returning Lead Instructor roles and future management internships. You'll spend your summer outside, building skills that translate directly to education, recreation, and leadership careers.

Customer Insights & Store Experience Associate

Hybrid (In-Store + Remote)
>💰 $15.00 – $16.50/hr🕐 10–15 hrs/week, flexible scheduling

Bucky Enterprises continuously evaluates how customers experience our stores. This role gathers structured feedback to improve layout, service speed, and product accessibility. You'll conduct in-store observation sessions, administer brief customer surveys, compile data into summary reports, and present findings to store management on a regular basis.

Responsibilities & Duties

  • Conduct structured observation sessions in-store, documenting customer traffic patterns and pain points
  • Administer short customer satisfaction surveys using tablets and paper forms
  • Enter survey data into spreadsheets and company reporting tools accurately
  • Prepare weekly summary reports highlighting trends, recurring issues, and customer suggestions
  • Present findings to store management during monthly review meetings
  • Collaborate with the regional Customer Experience team on special research projects

Who Fits This Role Best

  • Analytical mindset and comfort working with data, charts, and reports
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills
  • Discretion and professionalism when conducting in-store observations
  • Familiarity with spreadsheet software (Google Sheets or Excel)
  • Ability to work both independently (remote reporting) and in-store (observation shifts)
  • Interest in business strategy, consumer behavior, or data analytics

Why You'd Want This Job

This role provides exposure to business strategy, consumer behavior, and operational improvement — skills that are directly relevant to careers in business, economics, or data analytics. Students interested in understanding how companies make decisions based on customer data are encouraged to apply. High performers may be considered for future corporate internships.
